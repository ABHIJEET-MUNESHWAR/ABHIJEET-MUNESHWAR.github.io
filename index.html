Remove Google Redirects
Google adds redirection to all shared links to learn what links you click. This extension removes Google's redirection from all products of Google to protect your privacy & to prevent click tracking. The advantage is, shared links will load faster as they are no longer passed to Google for redirection. This extension works for below products of Google:
Google Search, Google Plus, Google Play, Google News, Google Books, Google Drive, Google Shopping, Google Bookmarks, Google History, Google Images, Google Groups and Google Maps. This extension is available at below links:
  • For Google Chrome
  • For Opera

Port Scanner
Port Scanner determines lively services on the host using TCP port interrogations. Port Scanner will check which TCP ports are listening. You can test these ports to see if they are running. It is recommended that any services which are not necessary be disabled. Use Port Scanner to scan individual ports to determine if the device is listening on that port. Port Scanner will analyze given IP address or URL for open ports to help you to secure it. This extension is available at below links:
  • For Google Chrome
  • For Mozilla Firefox
  • For Opera
